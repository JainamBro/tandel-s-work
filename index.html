Intorduction to SOLAR SYSTEM

The Solar System is a huge space family consisting of the SUN, eight planets , dwarf planets like Pluto , moons , asteroids, meteoroids , and comets . All these objects are held together by the SUN's GRAVITY.

It is important to study the Solar System beacuse it helps us understand how planets formed, how earth suppoers life, and how space phenomena affet out daily lives.

The SUN

The Sun is the center of the Solar System and the most important member. It is a massive ball of hot gases, mainly consisting of hydrogen and helium. The Sun provide heat and light , which make life possible on Earth.

It also controls the movement of planets and other objects throigh its strong gravitational pull . Without the Sun , there would be no energy , no seasons , and no life .

Planets of the solar system

In our Solar system there are total 9 planets which are divided into three parts as follows :-

  • Inner planets which are Mercury , Venus , Earth , Mars
  • Outer planets which are Jupiter , Saturn , Uranus , Neptune
  • Dwarf planet which is Pluto
