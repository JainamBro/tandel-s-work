Mercury

It's Position from Sun

Mercury is the closest planetto the Sun, orbiting at just 58 million km away. Because of this, it completes orbits faster than any other planet

Size of the planet

It is the smallest planet in our Solar System, with a diameter of 4,880 km , radius of 2,440 km , and a mass of 3.3 × 1023 kg

Atmosphere of the planet

Mercury has an extremely thin exosphere, containing traces of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium. It cannot trap heat, so temperatures swing from –173°C at night to +427°C during the day
