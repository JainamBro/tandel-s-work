VENUS

It's Position from Sun

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, orbiting at 108 million km away. It is often called Earth’s twin due to its similar size

Size of the planet

The Diameter of this planet is 12,104 km , radius 6,052 km , mass 4.87 × 1024kg.

Atmosphere of the planet

The Atmospher is Very thick, made of 96% carbon dioxide and clouds of sulfuric acid. This creates a powerful greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet (~465°C).

Gravity on the Planet

The Gravitaional pull on this is planet is of 8.87 m/s2 which is approximately 90% of Earth's Gravitaionalpull.

Number of moons of the planet

It also has No Moons.

Intesting Fact about this is that planet

Venus spins in the opposite direction of most planets (retrograde), and a single day is longer than its year.

Orbital Length of the planet

The orbital length of this planet is 67 million miles (108 million km).

Orbital Period(Number of days)

The total number of days on this planets are 225 Earth days.
